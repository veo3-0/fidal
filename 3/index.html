Direct Tax Provisions Expert Level

Under the Direct Tax Code (DTC) 2025, which category of taxpayers is eliminated compared to the Income Tax Act, 1961?
Resident and Non-Resident
Resident but Not Ordinarily Resident (RNOR)
Non-Resident and RNOR
All categories remain the same
Correct Answer: b) RNOR is removed under DTC, simplifying to only Resident/Non-Resident classifications.
A taxpayer conceals income of ₹50 lakhs and claims false deductions of ₹20 lakhs. What is the minimum penalty under Section 270A for underreporting income?
50% of the tax evaded
100% of the tax evaded
200% of the tax evaded
No penalty if corrected before assessment
Correct Answer: a) 50% for underreporting; 200% if misreporting is proven.
In international taxation, which BEPS (Base Erosion and Profit Shifting) Action mandates country-by-country reporting for MNCs with revenue exceeding ₹5,500 crores?
Action 5
Action 13
Action 15
Action 21
Correct Answer: b) BEPS Action 13 requires CBCR for large MNCs.
Under the DTC 2025, what is the tax rate for high earners (income above ₹5 crores)?
30% + surcharge
35% flat
37% including cess
40% for non-residents
Correct Answer: b) DTC proposes a flat 35% rate for high earners, unlike the current slab system.
Which of the following is not eligible for deduction under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act?
ELSS mutual funds
Children's tuition fees
Credit card payments
Principal repayment of home loan
Correct Answer: c) Credit card payments are not eligible for 80C deduction.

International Taxation Advanced

The "Principal Purpose Test" (PPT) under MLI (Multilateral Instrument) is designed to combat:
Transfer pricing manipulation
Treaty shopping
Permanent establishment avoidance
Thin capitalization
Correct Answer: b) PPT prevents treaty shopping by examining the principal purpose of transactions.
Under the OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines, which method is considered the "default" for determining ALP?
Comparable Uncontrolled Price (CUP)
Resale Price Method
Transactional Net Margin Method (TNMM)
Profit Split Method
Correct Answer: c) TNMM is the most commonly used method as per OECD guidelines.
